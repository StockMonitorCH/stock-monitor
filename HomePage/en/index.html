Overview

📊 Portfolio Overview

Complete overview with average cost basis, daily value, current value, P&L, weight, sector assignment (GICS) and 52-week high/low. In one view: stocks, ETFs, cryptocurrencies and precious metals.
And much more.

Portfolio Assessment

📋 Portfolio Assessment — at a Glance

Instant overall assessment of your portfolio: how well diversified are you really? Stock Monitor automatically evaluates sector distribution, concentration risks and strengths — clearly summarised, without any manual effort. No AI, no cloud — purely rule-based on your own data.

  • GICS sector analysis: distribution and concentration risks
  • Strengths & weaknesses automatically identified
  • Regional diversification via the interactive world map
  • No API key, no account required
Portfolio Analysis

📈 Portfolio Performance

TWR and MWR (IRR) calculation.
Comparison with the most important benchmark indices (S&P 500, SMI, DAX and more).
Alpha, Beta, Sharpe Ratio and Max. Drawdown are calculated automatically.

AI Analysis

⚖️ AI Balance & Rebalancing

Optionally AI-powered rebalancing combines historical performance with analyst consensus (Strong Buy→Sell) and current news sentiment of the last 30 days. AI support can be disabled at any time. Targeted rebalancing with filtering by GICS sector or currency is supported.

Dividends

📅 Dividend Calendar

All dividend payments in your portfolio at a glance: ex-div dates, pay dates, dividend per share and expected total yield.
Sorted by next payment date.

Dividend History

💰 Dividend History & Growth Analysis

Detailed history of all dividend payments with automatically calculated growth rate, stability score and forecast. See at a glance how reliably a stock pays out.

  • Annual & multi-year dividend growth (CAGR)
  • Stability rating and payout analysis
  • Dividend forecast based on historical data
Visualization

🌍 Regional World Map

Interactive world map shows your geographic portfolio distribution. Click a marker to open position details including company address and website.

Portfolio Forecast

🎲 Monte Carlo Simulation

Stochastic portfolio forecast using Geometric Brownian Motion (GBM). Simulate 10,000 possible price paths and get realistic growth ranges for your portfolio — from bearish to bullish.

  • 10,000 simulation paths, configurable time horizon (1–30 years)
  • 5% / 50% / 95% percentile bands — Bear / Base / Bull case
  • Based on historical volatility & drift via NumPy / GBM
  • Directly integrated into the Portfolio Performance view
Market Valuation

📊 Excess CAPE Yield (ECY)

Is the stock market currently cheap or expensive — compared to safe bonds? The ECY (earnings yield minus real 10Y bond yield) gives a historically validated answer. With colour-coded valuation zones and chart since 1988.

  • 🟢 >3% attractive · 🟡 1–3% neutral · 🟠 0–1% elevated · 🔴 <0% expensive
  • No API key — data calculated directly via Yahoo Finance
  • Not a buy/sell signal — a long-term orientation value
Risk Analysis

⚖️ Sharpe, Alpha & Beta — calculated automatically

Institutional risk metrics for private investors: Sharpe Ratio separately for stocks, crypto and commodities. Jensen's Alpha and Beta vs. S&P 500.

  • Sharpe Ratio with assessment (Excellent / Good / …)
  • Alpha: excess return vs. benchmark
  • Beta: market risk coefficient per position
New in v5.4.0

🔗 Correlation Matrix

See at a glance how strongly your positions are correlated with each other — and where real diversification lies. The correlation matrix calculates the Pearson correlation of daily returns of all portfolio positions. Freely selectable time period.

  • 🟢 Green → +1: move together · ⬜ Grey ≈ 0: independent · 🔴 Red → −1: move opposite
  • Period: 1M / 3M / 6M / YTD / 1Y / 2Y / 5Y freely selectable
  • Export as PDF, XLSX and ODS
  • No API key, no additional internet connection required for calculation
Right at Startup

🔀 Comparison Charts — 2 to 6 Stocks at Once

The comparison chart is visible right at app startup. Normalized scale so you immediately see who's really ahead — regardless of absolute price levels.

  • 2–6 symbols simultaneously on normalized basis (100 = start)

  • Y-axis lock & session persistence of symbols

Data Import

📥 CSV Import — with Guided Column Mapping

Known bank format? Just import. Unknown format? Stock Monitor auto-detects columns — or walks you through an interactive mapping wizard step by step. No troubleshooting, no manual tweaks.

  • Swissquote — fully supported including currency conversion & FX rates
  • DeGiro, Comdirect / ING, Flatex, Interactive Brokers, Trading 212 and more
  • Unknown format? Guided column mapping — you click, Stock Monitor understands
  • Buys, sells, splits and dividends automatically detected & correctly assigned
  • Cryptocurrencies and precious metals are automatically mapped correctly
Chart Detail

🕐 Market Hours & Holidays

Directly in the zoomed chart: Is the market currently open or closed? Stock Monitor shows opening hours, pre-market, after-hours and all market holidays — for over 30 exchanges worldwide, automatically detected from the ticker symbol.

  • 🟢 / 🔴 / 🟡 Real-time status — open, closed, pre-market or after-hours
  • Local exchange time and your local time at a glance
  • All market holidays for the current year — including next opening day
  • 30+ exchanges: NYSE/NASDAQ, SIX, XETRA, LSE, Euronext, Tokyo, Hong Kong, ASX and more
  • Cryptocurrencies: 24/7 — always open

About the Developer

Timm — Developer & Private Investor

I'm a private investor who spent years trying different portfolio tools — either the features I needed weren't there, the interface was too complex, or the CSV import didn't work as expected. On top of that came the constant manual updating of prices, splits and dividends. So I developed Stock Monitor — with automatic price data via Yahoo Finance. Your portfolio stays up to date at all times, without any manual maintenance. When colleagues saw the app and wanted to use it too, I decided to release it as an open-source project.
